ABOUT CAFE GATO

Founded by Maximo and Kaylin Mugica-Drumm, Cafe Gato is a unique space where coffee lovers and cat enthusiasts can come together and enjoy a cozy, welcoming environment. Our vision is to create a warm and inviting cafe for our guests while providing a separate, dedicated space for our feline friends. This allows you to savor your favorite beverages while enjoying the delightful company of cats
